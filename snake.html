The rules

  • Don't run the snake into the wall, or his own tail: you die.
  • Use your cursor keys: up, left, right, and down.
  • For two players game use cursor keys: W, S, A, D.
  • Eat the RED apples to gain points.
  • You will need to click "New Game" button to start a new game.
